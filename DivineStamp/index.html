About Divinestamp

Precision Manufacturing
from India's Auto Hub

Divinestamp Private Limited is a modern precision aluminium component manufacturing company established in February 2025 in Hosur, Tamil Nadu — one of India's fastest-growing automotive industrial hubs.

We specialize in manufacturing Front Fork Outer Tubes — critical structural components in two-wheeler front suspension systems — using advanced VMC machining technology and high-grade aluminium alloys.

"From raw aluminium to precision-engineered automotive components — every step is driven by engineering excellence and an uncompromising commitment to quality."

Our Hosur facility runs 2 advanced VMC machines, aluminium boiler processing, and precision forming equipment, operated by a team of 70+ skilled engineers and operators.
